What We Offer


We offer various types of services, specializing in braids.
Most of the types of braids for black hair were invented to keep the wild and unruly hair at bay.
Besides creating a neat hairstyle that can last for days, braids for black hair keep the locks from breaking.

So here are some of the styles we serve:

  • Braids
  • Cornrow Braids
  • Box Braids
  • Faux Locs
  • Havana (Marley) Twists
  • Deep Conditioning Treatment
  • Extensions
